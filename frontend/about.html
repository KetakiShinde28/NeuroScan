About NeuroScan

NeuroScan is an advanced AI-driven platform designed to detect and analyze brain tumors using MRI scans. Our deep learning model provides accurate tumor classification, localization, severity estimation, and growth prediction.

What We Offer

AI-Powered Tumor Detection

Our model accurately detects Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary tumors, and healthy brain scans.

Grad-CAM Heatmaps

Visualizes tumor regions in MRI scans for better interpretability.

Tumor Severity & Growth Prediction

Estimates tumor size and forecasts future growth over time.

Our Mission

NeuroScan aims to assist doctors, radiologists, and researchers by providing AI-based insights for early diagnosis and treatment planning.
